A Legacy of Compassion and Service

The Francis & Felicia Umenyi Foundation (FFUF) was established in loving memory of Mr. Francis and Mrs. Felicia Ify Umenyi—two extraordinary Nigerians whose lives embodied compassion, service, and faith in humanity.

“We must do something to help those who cannot access the care I have.” — Mrs. Felicia Umenyi

That promise now defines our mission: ensuring no woman or family faces illness or hardship alone.

Our Vision

A world where every woman and child, regardless of background, disability, or economic status, can live healthy, dignified, and fulfilled lives.

Our Mission

We work to advance women’s health, strengthen community health systems, promote disability inclusion, and ensure food and nutrition security across rural and underserved areas in Nigeria.

Our Focus Areas

💗

Women’s Health & Cancer Awareness

Awareness, prevention, and early detection of breast and gynecological cancers.

🏥

Rural Health & PHC Adoption

Adoption and revitalization of primary health centers, starting with Gosa PHC in Abuja.

🍎

Nutrition & Food Security

Food drives and nutrition education for women and children facing hunger.

📚

Education, WASH & Child Health

Health education, menstrual hygiene, and sanitation programs in schools.

♿

Disability Inclusion

Rehabilitation, healthcare access, and advocacy for persons with disabilities.

Our Strategy

We create lasting impact through strengthening existing community structures, building partnerships with government, NGOs, and private sector organizations, and empowering women and communities as agents of change.
