About us

A Legacy of Compassion and Service

The Francis & Felicia Umenyi Foundation (FFUF) was established in loving memory of Mr. Francis and Mrs. Felicia Ify Umenyi—two extraordinary Nigerians whose lives embodied compassion, service, and faith in humanity.

“We must do something to help those who cannot access the care I have.” — Mrs. Felicia Umenyi

That promise now defines our mission: ensuring no woman or family faces illness or hardship alone.

Our Vision

A world where every woman and child, regardless of background, disability, or economic status, can live healthy, dignified, and fulfilled lives.

Our Mission

We work to advance women’s health, strengthen community health systems, promote disability inclusion, and ensure food and nutrition security across rural and underserved areas in Nigeria.
